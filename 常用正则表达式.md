### 手机号正则校验

````java
String phoneRegex = "^((13[0-9])|(14[5,7,9])|(15([0-3]|[5-9]))|(166)|(17[0,1,3,5,6,7,8])|(18[0-9])|(19[8|9]))\\d{8}$";
````
